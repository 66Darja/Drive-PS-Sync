Privacy Policy for Drive PS Sync

Drive PS Sync is a personal tool used exclusively by the developer to synchronize local files with Google Drive.

Information Accessed

The application accesses the user's own Google Drive account for the sole purpose of uploading, updating, or synchronizing files and folders.

Data Usage

No personal data is collected, stored, transmitted, or shared with any third party. All operations occur directly between the user's local system and their Google Drive account.

Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with external services, organizations, or individuals.

Security

The application relies on Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication system. No credentials or tokens are stored or transmitted beyond what is required for authentication.

Contact

This application is privately developed and used. For any questions, contact the developer directly.
